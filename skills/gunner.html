The Gunner skill tree can barrage enemies with powerful rockets and grenades from afar. Sustain can be a bit tricky with the Medkit being the primary mechanism to stay alive. 

Tier 1

Gun Mastery

Passively Boosts damage and attack speed with guns.

Snipe

Fires a powerful laser cannon

Medkit

Passively drop a healing item when attacking. The item can be looted by any player to recover some HP.

Tier 2

Bullet Dance

Shoot a barrage of bullets from above while midair, slowing your fall.

Focus

Self-buff that allows you to shoot three bullets. Boosts atk, lowers def.

Grenade

Lob a smoke grenade at the enemies, damaging them over time.

Tier 3

Point-Blank

Deals massive, rapid short-range damage in front of the gunner.

Precision

Boosts crit damage of self and nearby allies.

Tech Bracers

Boosts max EP and cast speed.

Tier 4

Rocket

Launch a rocket at the closest enemy.

Bomblobber

Auto-launches grenades, rockets, and snipes.

Destructive Snipe

Snipe detonates grenades, dealing massive damage to nearby enemies.
